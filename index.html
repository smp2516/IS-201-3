Student, Dog Lover, Experience Seeker
Welcome to my life! I am a student at BYU who loves dogs, enjoys sports, and is always seeking new and fun experiences!

I graduated from Eureka High School in 2020 and have now been at Brigham Young University for two years. I'm currently Pre-Business but plan on applying for the Experience Design and Management Major. I am involved in a couple activites at BYU including: intramurals (coed volleyball) and clubs (K-Pop club). I also really enjoy attending sporting events and cheering on my fellow Cougars!!

Another main aspect of my life is that I am a huge dog enthusiast! I love so many breeds but am currently an owner of two Cavalier King Charles Spaniels. To learn more about this breed and my dogs specifically, click here to see my own website on them!

An additional interest in my life is sports! I grew up playing many including softball, volleyball, and basketball, but focused on cheerleading (both sideline and competitive) for the last six years before college. Although I mainly only play volleyball now, cheer had a huge impact on my earlier years. To learn more about my experience and outlook on cheerleading, click here. Watch below to join my team at one of our competitions!

One of the biggest joys for me in life is enjoying all sorts of experiences. Some of these include theme parks, concerts, festivals, travling/roadtrips, and so much more! I love planning and creating on my own, but I also really enjoy exploring and experiencing what others have put into the planning and creating of their own events. Ranging anywhere from smaller scale family activites to massive city special events, if it creates a memorable experience, I want to be there!
